About KNG Commodities LLP

KNG Commodities LLP is a Mumbai-based international brokerage firm established in 2017, specializing exclusively in edible oils. We act as independent intermediators, connecting reliable buyers and sellers across domestic and international markets.

Our focus is on transparency, disciplined execution, and long-term trade relationships within the global edible oil supply chain.

Our Core Expertise

Edible Oil Brokerage

Specialized intermediation for bulk and containerized edible oil shipments across global markets with structured trade processes.

Global Trade Coordination

End-to-end coordination from negotiation to contract alignment and shipment follow-ups.

Market Intelligence

Indicative pricing trends, supply-demand insights, and market updates to support commercial decision-making.
