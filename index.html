Trade Model, Scope & Disclaimer

KNG Commodities LLP operates strictly under a broker-only execution model. The firm acts solely as an intermediary facilitating communication, negotiation and execution between buyers and sellers in physical commodity transactions.

At no stage does KNG Commodities LLP purchase, sell, store, transport, insure, finance or take ownership of any goods. The firm does not assume title, custody or risk in respect of the commodities traded.

All commercial contracts are entered into directly between the buyer and seller. Pricing, quality parameters, shipment terms, payment conditions and dispute resolution mechanisms are mutually agreed by the contracting parties.

KNG Commodities LLP shall not be responsible or liable for:

  • Quality, quantity or condition of goods
  • Logistics, transportation or storage issues
  • Payment delays, defaults or credit risk
  • Performance failures by either counterparty
  • Market fluctuations or force majeure events

The firm‚Äôs role is limited to trade facilitation, coordination and execution support, without assuming any advisory, fiduciary or principal responsibility.

This broker-only operating structure ensures neutrality, transparency and long-term credibility across all market participants.
