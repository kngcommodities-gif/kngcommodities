About KNG Commodities LLP

KNG Commodities LLP is a Mumbai-based international edible oil and commodity brokerage firm, operating since 2017.

We facilitate domestic and international trade in edible oils, working with refiners, importers, exporters, and global trading houses.

Over the years, we have worked with reputed multinational and Indian organizations including Viterra India, Bunge India, Adani Group, Tata Group, and COFCO International, along with established local market participants.

We operate strictly as a broker and do not take ownership, storage, logistics, or financial exposure in any transaction.
